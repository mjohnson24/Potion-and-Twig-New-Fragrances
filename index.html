Spring Fragrances

Luxurious

Luxurious exudes sophistication and elegance with vibrant bursts of Italian bergamot and orange blossom mingling with the crispness of green leaves, evoking a sense of vitality and allure. As the fragrance evolves, delicate tuberose and Indian jasmine unfold, accompanied by the warmth of golden amber, Madagascar vanilla, and white musk, enveloping the senses in an irresistible embrace.

Fresh Wisteria Whispers

Fresh Wisteria Whispers evokes the delicate charm of a blooming garden with its crisp, aquatic top notes merging seamlessly with white florals and the timeless allure of rose, culminating in the enchanting essence of wisteria.

Island Getaway

Island Getaway transports you to paradise with its tropical coconut top notes, followed by a refreshing blend of white florals and citrus, all anchored by a warm base of amber and sweet undertones.

Summer Fragrances

Sunshine Burst

Sunshine Burst radiates with the vibrant essence of summer berries and sweet orange at the top, followed by a floral heart of white lily, clove, and jasmine, before settling into a base of fresh white petals, encapsulating the warmth and energy of a sunlit day.

Chic Allure

Chic Allure captivates with a harmonious blend of vibrant berries and mandarin orange, intertwining with a luxurious floral medley, before settling into a warm embrace of amber, caramel, vanilla, sandalwood, and pralines.

Seaside Serenity

Seaside Serenity embodies the essence of coastal tranquility with refreshing green and citrus notes, followed by a serene blend of coconut and floral accords, all anchored by a grounding base of musk and woody undertones.
